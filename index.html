Classical Mechanics

Department of Physics

Course Overview

This course develops the principles of classical mechanics from Newtonian mechanics to the Lagrangian and Hamiltonian formulations. Topics include oscillations, central force motion, rigid body dynamics, variational principles, and canonical transformations.
